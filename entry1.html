Fashion Show Success - July 2024

July 30, 2024

Our students dazzled the audience at the annual fashion show held at the Grand Ballroom. With their creativity and hard work, they showcased an incredible range of designs that impressed everyone. Special thanks to our guest judges for their valuable feedback and support.

The event was a grand success, featuring an array of innovative designs from our talented students. The evening was filled with vibrant energy as each model presented their unique style on the runway. The fashion show not only highlighted the students' skills but also provided them with invaluable exposure and experience.

We received numerous compliments from the attendees, and several media outlets covered the event, further amplifying our students' achievements. The feedback from the judges was overwhelmingly positive, praising the students for their professionalism and creativity.

We look forward to more such successful events in the future and are excited to see our students continue to excel in their modeling careers.
